Profile


Dr. ignatius kirabo
has been a major player in the farming sector,
of Uganda from 1993. working together with local & foreign organisations, he has been able to grow, learn & apply the benefits of farming & there main challenges. for this very reason, he sought a solution to improve the products and crop produced on Ugandan soil. and also improve on the market standard world wide.
At Tenrou farming,
he has been able to grow various crops, animals, and also deveelop one of the best processing facilities in the country. through the NAADS program, and the ministry of Agric, he has reached out to a number of farmers, both begining and already large scale. so they can work together and learn more about better farming techniques.
